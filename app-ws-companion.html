App description

Companion app for fantasy skirmish board game Whack & Slaughter. Whack & Slaughter is a small scale skirmish system that plays quick and is meant to provide countless hours of fun.

App features:
  • list of schools and skills
  • character creation and sheet

Find out more about board game on game designer's page or on boardgamegeek.com
