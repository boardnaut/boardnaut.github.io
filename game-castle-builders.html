Castle Builders

Quick to play board game where you build castle for your king

by Björn Jansson & Daniel Johansson
Game description

The king has ordered two builders to build a new castle for him. You are one of them. "The award you will be given will be in proportion to the quality of your work", the king said. "The quality of your work", you think. He doesn't care about the quality, he only cares for what he can see with his eye... You will have to build the top of the towers, for that is where the king will look first. And if you can show the king how much you have built, you will surely be given an extra award.

Castle Builders is quick board game where players are competing to build most important places in new castle. Players use various build gaming pieces to directly build castle and special gaming pieces to damage build efforts of opponent.

Game modes:
  • training game to play against AI
  • hot-seat multiplayer for two player game using one device
